An overview of where you can find solarpunk and where solarpunk can find you.

WHERE TO NOW?

Solarpunk has a long way to go. We are a loose, decentralized movement, and while that provides plenty of space for development of novel ideas and refinement of personal aesthetics/philosophy within solarpunk, there is also no unified front. We are grassroots, and many patches stand apart from others. We need to bridge that gap.

What you can do

  • eco consciousness: reduce, reuse, reuse again!
    • Old tins/containers as seed starters or planters

    • Try hydroponic gardening if you have the resources; it takes less water and time than traditional soil gardening.
  • learning to code -> coding your life -> taking back your tech
    • solarpunk LOVES tech. but it loves handmade tech. if you’re able, try your hand at coding. if you have access to a 3D printer, through say a local business or library, try making things you need like a phone stand!
    • customize the tech in your life. hack it apart and put it back together as you envision it

  • try to go solar! Hit local yard sales for secondhand tools and hack stuff together with scrap materials. If you can get your hands on a solar panel, revel in your amazingness!
